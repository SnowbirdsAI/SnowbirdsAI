To ensure clarity and aid understanding, we define the following terms as used in this Privacy Policy:

  • Company ("We", "Us", "Our"): Refers to Snowbirds Ai, Surrey, BC, Canada, which is responsible for your information under this Privacy Policy.
  • Country: The location where Snowbirds Ai or its owners/founders are based, in this case, Canada.
  • Customer: Any company, organization, or person that uses Snowbirds Ai Service to manage relationships with consumers or service users.
  • Device: Any internet-connected device such as a phone, tablet, computer that can be used to access Snowbirds Ai and use its services.

  • Third-party service: Advertisers, contest sponsors, promotional and marketing partners, and others whose products or services may interest you.

  • You: A person or entity registered with Snowbirds Ai to use the Services.

Data Practices

Data Collection and Use

Snowbirds Ai, through its services including LangSpur, collects various types of data to enhance functionality, ensure security, and provide personalized experiences. All data is encrypted in transit and transmitted securely via the Internet standard https protocol and SDK libraries provided by third-party service providers.

What Data We Collect and How

  • Direct Collection: Information directly from you when you use our services, like when you register for an account, interact with our AI, or participate in surveys. This includes contact information, device identifiers, and interaction data.
  • Automated Collection Technologies: Our services automatically collect certain information from your device to improve app performance and user experience. This includes technical information about your device, app usage patterns, and performance metrics.
  • Information from Third Parties: We may receive information about you from third-party partners and platforms that enhance our services.

Data Types and Usage

  • Personal Information: Including names, email addresses, and user IDs, collected for account management and app functionality. This collection is essential for service provision.
  • Messages: Other in-app messages are collected to support app functionality.
  • Photos and Videos: Users have the option to provide photos, which are used for app functionality and account management. Additionally, when signing up with a Google account, the URL of the profile image registered with Google is used.
  • Audio Files and Voice Inputs: User voice input for app features is temporarily processed to facilitate interaction with AI services. Improve user experience, especially by using voice input to communicate with AI. Text-based user conversations can also be converted to speech via text-to-speech (TTS) capabilities or converted to audio files by AI speech generation service providers. Snowbirds Ai, including the LangSpur Service, temporarily processes your voice to provide the Service, but does not permanently record or store your voice.
  • App Info and Performance: This includes crash logs, diagnostics, and other app performance data, collected for functionality improvements, analytics, and ensuring security.
  • App Activity: Such as app interactions and other actions, collected to enhance app functionality, analytics, and for security measures.
  • Device IDs: Used for app functionality and personalized advertising, with mandatory collection for these purposes.

App Data Collection Technologies

Our service employs advanced technologies for data collection and performance monitoring to provide personalized experiences and improve app functionality. This approach allows for responsible data collection aligned with user preferences.

Types of Data Collected and Purposes

  • App Usage Data: Data on user interactions, engagement insights, and app performance metrics to enhance functionality and user experience.
  • Performance Metrics: Diagnostics such as crash logs and interaction speeds to identify and address issues for a seamless app experience.

Users can customize their data collection settings within the app, opting in or out based on personal preferences.

User Rights and Data Security

User Rights and Choices

At Snowbirds Ai, we recognize and respect your rights regarding your personal data. This section outlines the rights you have over your data and how you can exercise them:

  • Right to Know and Access: You have the right to inquire about the personal information we collect, use, share, and store about you. Upon request, we can provide you with a copy of this information.
  • Right to Correction: If you believe that any personal data we hold about you is incorrect or incomplete, you have the right to request its correction.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data from our systems, subject to certain exceptions prescribed by law.
  • Right to Opt-Out: For residents of jurisdictions with opt-out rights, such as California under the CCPA, you have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal data to third parties.
  • Data Portability: In certain jurisdictions, you have the right to receive a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and/or request us to transmit this data to another service provider (where technically feasible).
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided in this policy. Depending on your location, you may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority if you are unhappy with our data protection practices.

Data Deletion

Users have control over their data, including options for deletion. Snowbirds Ai provides mechanisms for users to delete of their account and associated data, supporting user empowerment and privacy.

  • Delete App Account: Users can find out how to delete their account and related data through the account deletion procedure, a dedicated page: Account Deletion Procedure.
  • Manage App Data: Users can directly delete user-generated data or delete their accounts through services provided by Snowbirds Ai. If necessary, you may request assistance with deletion via our contact email.

Our commitment to data security includes providing users with the option to delete user-generated data, aligning with best practices for data protection.

Data Security and Retention

At Snowbirds Ai, we prioritize the security of your personal data. Our comprehensive security strategy includes technical and administrative measures designed to protect data against unauthorized access, use, alteration, and destruction. We utilize encryption, firewalls, access controls, and secure software development practices. Our infrastructure is built on reliable cloud platforms that offer advanced security features, ensuring that your data is securely processed and stored.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, as required by law, or as needed to provide services to our users. The retention period varies depending on the nature of the data and the reasons for its collection. For example, account information is retained for as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Once the retention period expires, personal data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, Snowbirds Ai will promptly notify affected users and regulatory authorities, in compliance with legal requirements. The notification will detail the nature of the breach, the categories of data involved, and the actions taken to address the breach.

Compliance and International Data Transfers

International Data Transfers and Compliance

We transfer personal data across borders in compliance with international data protection laws. This includes the use of Standard Contractual Clauses for data transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), ensuring the protection of your personal information regardless of where it is processed.

In recognition of our global user base, Snowbirds Ai commits to adhering to international data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users within the European Union, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for residents of California, United States. This commitment ensures the protection of your personal data regardless of your geographic location.

Compliance with Canadian Privacy Laws

Snowbirds Ai is committed to adhering to Canada's privacy laws, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA). These laws set the standards for the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information in a manner that recognizes the right of privacy of individuals with respect to their personal information.

We also ensure compliance with specific municipal regulations applicable in Surrey, BC, where our company is headquartered. Our privacy practices are designed to meet or exceed the requirements of these laws, providing our users with a high level of data protection and security.
